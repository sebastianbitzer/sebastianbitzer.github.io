Biography

Since April 2022, I am a doctoral researcher at the Technical University of Munich in the group for coding and cryptography (COD). Prof. Antonia Wachter-Zeh is my supervisor and Violetta Weger my mentor.

I received my B.Sc and M.Sc degree in Electrical Engineering from Ulm University in 2018 and 2021, respectively. I particularly enjoyed working with Prof. Martin Bossert.
