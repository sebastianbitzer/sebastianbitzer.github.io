Munich Workshop on Coding and Cryptography (MWCC) 2024

The MWCC took place at the TUM campus on April 8 to 10, 2024. The workshop covered several aspects of coding theory, with a special emphasis on topics related to post-quantum cryptography and privacy-preserving machine learning. Sixteen leading reaserchers from various universities were invited to present their research.

Co-Chairs: Rawad Bitar, Sebastian Bitzer, Christoph Hofmeister, and Antonia Wachter-Zeh.
